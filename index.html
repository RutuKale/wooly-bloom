About Us

Our Story 💕

Wooly Bloom began in a small studio in Mumbai, where one woman's love for crochet grew into a brand that brings joy to thousands.

👩‍🎨

Sneha – Founder & Artist

The Artist

Meet the Maker

Hi! I'm Sneha, the heart and hands behind Wooly Bloom. I started crocheting at 16 when my grandmother taught me my first stitch. What began as a rainy-day hobby quickly became my passion — and eventually my purpose.

In 2021, I started gifting custom bouquets to friends and the response was overwhelming. People were moved by receiving something so carefully handmade — something that would never wilt.

Today, Wooly Bloom ships hundreds of handmade pieces across India every month, and I still make every single one myself — stitch by stitch, with love.

🎯

Our Mission

To make the world warmer, one stitch at a time. We believe handmade gifts carry an energy no mass-produced product ever can — the time, care, and love woven into every piece.

🌟

Our Vision

To become India's most loved handmade gifting brand — where every celebration is made more beautiful by a piece of Wooly Bloom art.
